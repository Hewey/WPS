Header

Target Audience

Designed to grab attention and with the count down, I believe the target audience to be 20-30 year-olds interested in new emerging technology.

Client/User Needs

Because of the clear intent to catch the users attention one of the goals is to convey some information about the new technology they are working on. Another goal, made clear by the counter, being to build excitement and anticipation for this new technology.

Critique

The simplicity of the design serves the purpose to keep the message clear and to the point, avoiding confusion. The background image helps convey an idea of excitement and communicates an idea of the company being on the edge of a break through in technology. The increasing font size helps guide the attention to product of feature of said product. The products text being blue to communicate trust in said product. Because the products text is larger it allows for the message to be the most important part of the header allowings easier focus for the user.

News Feed

Target Audience

The target audience being gamers. Typically around the ages of 18-40.

Client/User Needs

The need here is to gain the users attention to get them to read a review. A user can come here to find reviews of recent games for their relevent platform.

Critique

The feed here is using a four column layout. The larger is being used for the games title and the smaller used as a quick discription. There are screenshots used behind the text to help grab the users attention and entice them to reading the review. Red is used to highlight the fact that these are written reviews. There are colored tags indicating what platform the game review is on. The color in this design seems to be just that color. There doesn't really seem to be any signifigance to the colors other than the fact that they are colors.
